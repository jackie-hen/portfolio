THE PRODUCT

As the pace of life quickens with each passing day, sharing or creating a meal has become a rare and fleeting event. Bliss Bowl aims to reclaim the intimate and meaningful dining experience by combining traditional craftsmanship with futuristic technology.

Deliverable

Product photos and prototype, high quality renderings,
campaign film and concept, branding package

May 2024

Founded in 2053, the Bliss Bowl is crafted from ceramic and hardwood to evoke a reverence for food and foster an elevated connection between people and their meals. Containing only essentials: three bowls, one serving plate, and one set of chopsticks, customers are guaranteed an intentional relationship with their food. These dishes are a testament to a future where food is more than sustenance; it's a source of nourishment for the body and soul. In a world where the communal spirit of breaking bread has all but been replaced, you don’t have to be disconnected.

Sustainable design

Keeping in line with the philosophy of the project, the assembly of the AlWadi desert pack is easily automated. Caring for a AlWadi pack is just as easy, as all parts can be mended, replaced or patched. That's why no major component is dependent through adhesion or permanent connection to any other piece. It's all outlined in the AlWadi Assembly manual.
